## My Top Recommendations

### If you want the BEST shot at winning overall:
→ **The Agora** — It screams "toolkit for civilization" louder than anything else.
  A civilization without trust is just a collection of strangers with guns.

### If you want to be remembered:
→ **Frontier Pulse** — Judges will literally say "I've never seen anything like this."
  It's art meets data meets gameplay.

### If you want to play it safe with high utility:
→ **Meridian** — The most "obviously useful" tool. Every serious player would want it.

### Best combination if building two:
→ **The Agora + Frontier Pulse** — Covers Utility/Technical AND Creative/Weirdest.
